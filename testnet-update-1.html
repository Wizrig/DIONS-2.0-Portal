Where We Started: Legacy DIONS Under IOCoin

Legacy IOCoin DIONS was already real infrastructure, not a blank slate: alias identity + alias data, messaging APIs, file/data upload-download-transfer flows, AES-256 + RSA-4096 cryptography, shade/privacy payment paths, and operational PoS chain behavior.

DIONS 2.0 is a fundamental protocol upgrade to make those capabilities deterministic, scalable, execution-ready (EVM + SVM), and hardened for modern adversarial/runtime conditions.

Critical Failure Classes Found and Corrected

  • FIXED: Sender/nonce/auth mismatch class across wallet, mempool, and validation (determinism failures).
  • FIXED: Metered DIONS mempool contamination/reject-loop class impacting liveness.
  • FIXED: P2P large payload relay defect (EAGAIN/EWOULDBLOCK treated as fatal) causing divergence risk.
  • FIXED: Quantum/hybrid validation consistency defects from earlier RC iterations.
